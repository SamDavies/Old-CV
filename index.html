Thing - Photo Sharing Social Network

Founder and creator of the social network Thing, available on the iOS app store. Users can create events (things) and share images.

Thing helps you find the best student events and night clubs with the potential to expand beyond the student target market. It is currently available on the iOS App Store.

For my 4th year honours project, I implemented a newsfeed ordering algorithm inspired by Facebook’s EdgeRank and a recommender engine to predict future ‘Things’ using Spark. In the same way that Netflix recommends movies, Thing is able to learn what Things a user likes and push these to the top of their newsfeed.

ORBIT - Factset Online Report Builder Interface

My internship at Factset in London was to design a web interface in Angular JS, allowing non technical product developers to generate data reports. This replaced many steps in the development of all reports at Factset by highly automating the process. I also pioneered behaviour driven development in the company, providing a presentation and tutoring the engineers. They have since offered me a full time position as a Software Engineer.

SmartLock

A team project to design and implement a smart door lock for the IoT. The door can be remotely locked and unlocked via the smart phone apps, whilst still allowing the use of a physical key. You can enable your friends to lock and unlock your Smart Locks, all controlled through apps for iOS, Android and Web. Machine learning techniques are being used to classify malicious entries.

Autonomous Robot Football

I led a team of 7, creating robots which played foosball autonomously against opposition robots using a planner and vision system connected to an overhead camera. The final tournament was attended by industry experts from Google, Amazon, IBM and Accenture.

Creating pages manually
